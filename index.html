SUCCESS IS A RISK WORTH TAKING

Why "The Maze"?

Life often feels like a maze—dead ends, wrong turns, long tunnels. FGO isn’t here to give you shortcuts. It’s here to remind you that every twist you take builds who you are becoming. The goal isn’t to escape the maze… it’s to understand it.
